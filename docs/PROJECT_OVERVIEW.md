# The Wayfarer Project Overview

## 1. Introduction

The Wayfarer is an interactive simulation project focused on exploring systems thinking, decision-making, and the relationship between individual actions and larger-scale social development.

The project explores how choices made by individuals, communities, and civilizations can create long-term consequences through time.

---

## 2. Vision

The Wayfarer aims to create an environment where participants can experience complex systems through interactive simulation.

Instead of focusing only on traditional game objectives, the project explores:

- Cause and effect relationships
- Long-term consequences of decisions
- Interactions between different scales of society
- The development of human understanding through exploration

---

## 3. Core Concept

The project is based on the idea that events do not exist independently.

Individual decisions may influence:

Individual level

↓

Community level

↓

Regional level

↓

National level

↓

Global civilization level

Through time, these interactions create complex historical development paths.

---

## 4. Development Status

The Wayfarer is currently in the conceptual research and documentation phase.

Current focus:

- Organizing project philosophy
- Defining system concepts
- Building documentation structure
- Exploring future prototype possibilities

---

## 5. Public and Private Scope

The public repository contains:

- Project vision
- General concepts
- Development progress
- Collaboration guidelines

Certain creative assets, unpublished designs, detailed narrative structures, and proprietary concepts remain protected as private intellectual property of the project founder.

---

## 6. Long-Term Goal

The long-term goal of The Wayfarer is to explore new approaches to interactive learning, simulation, and human understanding of complex systems.

The project welcomes constructive discussion and collaboration while respecting intellectual property rights and contributor ownership.
